Web speech API demo: hands free recipes

This demo showcases the new speech recognition and synthesis APIs. It reads out steps in a recipe using speech synthesis, and allows the user to navigate steps with voice recognition. This means they can cook a recipe from their phone without getting it dirty.

You will need a microphone and speakers. You may also need to give the application permission to listen to your microphone.

Output

Step three

Chop the chorizo into chunks roughly 1cm wide

Commands

Next, continue
Move ahead one step
Back, previous
Move back one step
Repeat, again, what
Stops speaking and begins again

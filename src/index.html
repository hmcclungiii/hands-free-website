Recipe: Caldo Verde

This delicious chorizo-and-cabbage soup is a classic of Portugese cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 chorizo, sliced into thick rounds
  • 5 potatoes, diced
  • 2 pints of chicken stock
  • a large bunch of dark greens - cavolo nero works well - chopped finely
  • smoked paprika
  • 2 bay leaves
  • salt and pepper, to taste

Method

  1. Fry the onions and garlic in the olive oil until soft.
  2. Add the chorizo.
  3. Fry the onions and sausage for a few more minutes and then add the potatoes.
  4. Add the stock, seasoning and bay leaves, and cook until the potatoes are soft.
  5. When the potatoes are ready, mash them into the broth to make a thick base. Blanch the greens in boiling water,, drain, then add to the simmering broth.
  6. Add as much cabbage as the broth will allow.
  7. Simmer for a few minutes. The soup will turn an enticing shade of green.
